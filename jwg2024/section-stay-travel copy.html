Universitas Negeri Surabaya is hosting the 13rd Assises de la Coopération Franco-Indonésienne which takes place alternately in France and Indonesia. More than 250 French and Indonesian participants attended the 12th Assises at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France The 13rd th Assises de la Coopération Franco-Indonésienne is co-organised by the French Institute of Indonesia and the Indonesian Ministry of Education (KEMDIKBUDRISTEK), the National Agency for Research and Innovation (BRIN) under the auspices of the French Embassy in Indonesia and the Indonesian Embassy in France. The coordination is ensured by the Universitas Negeri Surabaya. The objectives of the JWG are multiple:

  • Strengthen the networks between French and Indonesian actors
  • Promote opportunities for cooperation between institutions in both countries
  • Evaluate cooperative activities and define future directions
  • Identify the priorities
  • Participate in presentations, roundtables and workshops facilitating the exchange of information and good practices of cooperation;
  • Create new partnerships and consortia.

English will be the working language in all sessions, unless all participants happen to be fluent in either French or Indonesian.

Location

The 2024 edition will take place at the Universitas Negeri Surabaya, Surabaya, Indonesia. See the Stay and Travel section for more information.
