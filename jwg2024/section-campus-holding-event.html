Universitas Negeri Surabaya at A Glance

Unesa is one of the State Universities in Surabaya predicated A, ranked by National Accreditation Board of Higher Education, Republic of Indonesia. As one of the top universities in Indonesia, we provide interdisciplinary research and offer 110 study programs from bachelor to doctoral degrees. We open all departments to foreign students, delivered both in Indonesian language and a few in English.

Life at UNESA

Dormitory

Unesa has prepared University Dormitory for accommodation offer for all commencing students who are new to Surabaya, Indonesia. We are supported with year-round social, academic, wellbeing and sporting activities and a team of professional staff at each site to ease your transition into university life.

Grocery

Unesa has prepared University Dormitory for accommodation Unesa has a Grocery Store called KOCIKA that is located both in Ketintang and Lidah Wetan. It provides them with easy access to essential food items, beverages, snacks, even household items.

Food Court

Various food and drinks are available at Food Court Unesa. Almost each faculty has a Food Court that provides Indonesian or Traditional Food, sometimes it has International Snacks like Korean Noodles, Teriyaki Chicken and ect.

Transportation

If you want to see a sight in Unesa, you can go by bycycle that you can borrow it by giving your Student ID Card.

Sport and Recreation

Unesa has various sport and recreation with International Standards such as GOR Futsal, Basketball, Jogging Tracks, Tennis Field, and ect. There are also Merdeka Belajar Laboratory which is a recreation place in UNESA, in there you can enjoy the facility like study in a public space, you can also see various birds and some animals to release your stress after class.

Life at Surabaya

Heritage of Surabaya

Surabaya has full of History and Heritage. It has played a significant role in the country's development and has a vibrant cultural heritage that reflects its diverse influences. There are some historical buildings that can be enjoyed.

Shopping Center

As the Second Mega City in Indonesia, offers a diverse range of shopping experiences, from bustling traditional markets to modern shopping centers. For fun fact, some of Shopping malls in Surabaya are also the largest shopping malls in Indonesia and Asia.

Public Space

Surabaya has various public spaces that cater to the diverse needs and interests of its residents and visitors. Because Surabaya is the Second Largest City in Indonesia, so there are so many urban park that can use by citizens to relax and enjoy the citylight also.

Nature Destination

Although Surabaya is the 2nd biggest city in Indonesia, Surabaya also has nature destinations to visit such as Mangrove Forest, Various Park in Surabaya those are Bungkul Park, Pakal Park, and ect. If you want to experience Stunning Sunrise Views on a Mount, you may go to Mount Bromo which is 3 hours away from Surabaya.
